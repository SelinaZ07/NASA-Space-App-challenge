What we used to make this mindmap?

We looked into Nasa's 2025 awareness list in Nasa's Task Book website. We summarized all the research papers mentioned on the awareness list from May 2025 to now. We then used machine learning models to categorize the research papers into 6 categories and used LLM models to summarize each category. Users can click on the planets to view a summary of that category. We hope this map can help you stay up to date with Nasa's most recent research focuses. Additionally, this may provide insights to the areas that lack attention, which could be great path to dive into in the future.

All the resources we used on this page is from this link: NASA task book
