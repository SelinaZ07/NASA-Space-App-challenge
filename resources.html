NASA Life Sciences Portal:

NASA's Human Research Program (HRP) conducts research and develops technologies that allow humans to travel safely and productively in space. The Program uses evidence from data collected on astronauts, as well as other supporting studies. These data are stored in the research data repository, Life Sciences Data Archive (LSDA).

NASA Life Sciences Library:

The NASA Space Life Sciences Library (NSLSL) consolidates global space life sciences literature into a single database to support research that addresses the effects of the space environment on biological systems.The purpose of the library is to consolidate world-wide, peer-reviewed articles, technical publications, dissertations, and patent publications related to Space Life Science research into a singular, global, central repository that is accessible to the public.
